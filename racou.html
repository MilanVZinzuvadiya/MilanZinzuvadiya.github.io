Info

Sound Quality is crucially important for pleasant atmosphere. Advanced acoustical physics derives an important link between sound quality and reverberation time. Further, it is established that by making certain modifications in sound reflective surfaces, we can achieve standard reverberation time which ultimately leads to better sound quality.

RAcou - A desktop software application, can measure quality of sound by taking data of common surfaces of closed spaces. It makes suggestions to improve sound quality of the room.
